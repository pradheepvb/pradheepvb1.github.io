About Valorant

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter with unique agent abilities. Released by Riot Games, it focuses on strategic gameplay, teamwork, and sharp aim.

WE ARE VALORANT

The Agents

One of Valorant's defining features is its diverse roster of Agents. These characters possess distinct sets of four abilities, allowing for a wide range of playstyles and team compositions. From the fiery duelist Phoenix to the strategic controller Viper, mastering your chosen agent is key to success.

  • Duelists: Focus on engaging enemies and securing frags.
  • Controllers: Excel at cutting off enemy territory and setting up plays.
  • Initiators: Disrupt enemy defenses and pave the way for their team.
  • Sentinels: Specialize in locking down areas and providing defensive support.
